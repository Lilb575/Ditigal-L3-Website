Poverty in New Zealand

Poverty, in the simplest sense of words is a state where one lacks acess to basic needs such as food, clothing and shelter. Not being able to participate in activites or even medications for illness. Individuals in New Zealand have overcome poverty by following different pathways, including education, sports and enterpreneuship. The stories of Janet frame, Jonah Lomu and Alexia Hilbertidou Highlight how determination, skill, and oppportunity can help break the cycle of financial hardship.
